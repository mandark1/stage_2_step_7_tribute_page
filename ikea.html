IKEA was founded by Ingvar Kamprad in 1943 and came to life as a mail order catalogue business in the forested town of Älmhult, Sweden. Today, it is a global home furnishing brand that brings affordability, design and comfort to people all over the world. The IKEA vision is “To create a better everyday life for the many people.”, not just with their furniture but also with sustainability and the environment. Their business idea is “to offer a wide range of well-designed, functional home furnishing products at prices so low that as many people as possible will be able to afford them.”

The furniture is simplistic and minimalistic, much like most Scandinavian style interiors. I personally love that you can get such functional items at such low prices and they actually look nice in your home too. Also, who doesn't love a hot dog (or varmkorv in Swedish) as they're leaving IKEA, it's an absolute must!!
